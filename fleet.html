Our Fleet

Powerful & Diverse Fleet

Our fleet covers a wide range of equipment types, ensuring we can move freight of all shapes and sizes across the country.

Box Truck

Ideal for short-haul and regional deliveries. Our box trucks ensure quick transport of retail, furniture, and parcel shipments.

Reefer

Refrigerated trailers maintain temperature control for food, beverages, and pharmaceuticals, ensuring product safety.

Car Hauler

Specialized trailers designed for secure auto transport, whether moving single cars or multiple vehicles at once.

Power Only

Provides flexible solutions by connecting tractors with customer trailers, giving carriers more opportunities for freight.

Dry Van

The most versatile equipment, dry vans move general cargo efficiently across short and long-haul routes.

Flatbed

Built for oversized and heavy freight such as machinery, lumber, and construction materials requiring open-deck transport.
